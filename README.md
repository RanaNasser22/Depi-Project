##  Project Idea
The project is based on designing and building **smart residential cities** featuring:  
- Smart Home systems for full control.  
- Solar-powered communities to reduce costs and protect the environment.  
- A unified mobile app for residents.  
- **Digital Twin** technology as a USP for smarter city and home management.  
- Housing solutions for diverse segments (from mid-range units to luxury villas).  
- A safe, comfortable, and sustainable long-term living environment.

##  Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
- **Digital Twin for individuals:** For the first time in Egypt, homeowners can access a personal dashboard to monitor energy consumption, device health, predictive maintenance, and simulate expansions before execution.  
- **Integration with solar energy:** Not just smart homes or solar systems, but one connected ecosystem that tracks solar energy use, calculates savings, and alerts users about maintenance needs.  
- **Affordable for the middle class:** While competitors focus on luxury villas, Eterna delivers practical, accessible solutions for the middle class.  
- **Community-level Digital Twin:** Beyond individual homes, residents can monitor shared energy efficiency, infrastructure performance, and savings across the compound.  
- **Sustainability impact:** The system measures carbon footprint reductions and shows tangible results (e.g., ‚Äú20% less electricity = 30kg CO2 reduction this month‚Äù).
